## Laptop Market Prices 2026

Get the latest resale values for your used laptop in Kochi market.

**Dell Laptops:**

### **Factors Affecting Price**

- **Age**: 3-4 year depreciation
- **Condition**: Working vs damaged
- **Configuration**: RAM, SSD, processor
- **Market demand**: Popular models fetch better prices
