## E-Waste Disposal Fines & Penalties in Kochi

Understanding and avoiding e-waste disposal penalties in Kerala.

### **Legal Framework**

**Key Regulations:**
- E-Waste Management Rules 2016
- Kerala State Pollution Control Board (KSPCB) guidelines
- Environmental Protection Act penalties

### **Common Violations & Fines**

**Improper Disposal:**
- Individuals: Rs 5,000 - 25,000
- Small businesses: Rs 10,000 - 1,00,000
- Large corporations: Rs 50,000 - 5,00,000

**Data Protection Violations:**
- Data breach penalties: Up to Rs 5,00,000
- Non-compliance with DPDP Act: Rs 50,000 - 5,00,000

### **Compliance Requirements**

- Use authorized recyclers only
- Maintain disposal records
- Provide certificates of recycling
- Annual compliance reporting
