## DPDP Act 2023 Compliance for IT Disposal

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act mandates secure data handling during IT asset disposal.

### **Key Requirements**

**Data Erasure Standards:**
- Complete data destruction before disposal
- Certificate of destruction required
- Audit trail maintenance for 3 years

**Storage Media Handling:**
- Hard drives, SSDs, USB drives
- Mobile devices, tablets
- Backup tapes and optical media

### **Compliance Checklist**

- [ ] Data inventory before disposal
- [ ] Secure data destruction method
- [ ] Documentation and certificates
- [ ] Vendor compliance verification
- [ ] Staff training records
